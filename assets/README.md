# Social Media Images Required

Create these images for optimal social media sharing:

## 1. og-image.jpg

**Size:** 1200 x 630 pixels
**Format:** JPG (optimized, under 1MB)
**Usage:** Facebook, LinkedIn, general Open Graph

**Content Should Include:**

- Company logo
- Headline: "Vetted developers. No recruiters. Ship in 72 hours."
- Professional background (use your brand colors: #0a0e27, #00d9ff)
- Optional: Stats like "500+ Placements" or "72hr Average"

## 2. twitter-image.jpg

**Size:** 1200 x 600 pixels
**Format:** JPG (optimized, under 1MB)
**Usage:** Twitter/X Cards

**Content Should Include:**

- Company logo
- Headline: "Hire Stack Solutions"
- Tagline: "Global Talent Outsourcing"
- Clean, professional design

## Design Tools:

**Free Online Tools:**

- Canva: https://www.canva.com/
- Adobe Express: https://www.adobe.com/express/
- Figma: https://www.figma.com/

**Templates:**
Use "Open Graph Image" or "Twitter Card" templates

## Best Practices:

1. **Keep text large and readable** - Will be shown small on mobile
2. **Use high contrast** - Ensures readability
3. **Avoid small details** - They get lost when scaled down
4. **Test on multiple platforms** - Preview on Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
5. **Optimize file size** - Use tools like TinyPNG to compress

## Testing:

After uploading, test your images with:

- **Facebook Debugger:** https://developers.facebook.com/tools/debug/
- **Twitter Card Validator:** https://cards-dev.twitter.com/validator
- **LinkedIn Post Inspector:** https://www.linkedin.com/post-inspector/
